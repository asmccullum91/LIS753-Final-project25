Biography

  • Full Name: Harry James Potter
  • Birthday: July 31, 1980
  • Relatives: Lily Potter, James Potter, Sirius Black, Petunia Dursley, Vernon Dursley, Dudley Dursley
  • Pet: Hedwig the Owl

Harry James Potter is an orphan, who, on his eleventh birthday, learns he is a wizard. He attends Hogwarts, a school of magic, where he receives guidance from the headmaster Albus Dumbledore and becomes friends with Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger. Harry learns that during his infancy, the Dark wizard Lord Voldemort murdered his parents but was unable to kill him as well. The plot of the series revolves around Harry's struggle to adapt to the wizarding world and defeat Voldemort.
